Parent Information

"Free, no obligation, initial consultation available"

At Tableland Tutoring we can assess your child’s current level of skill in Literacy and Numeracy. We can identify any gaps between their current skill levels and the new C2C curriculum expectations for grade level. Our testing can also identify students who are under-achieving – these students have ability, but for some reason, are not achieving at their best.

While some students need help to ‘catch up’, others need extending because they learn quickly and easily. Our aim is to help your child learn to the best of their ability.

Our free, no obligation, initial consultation will begin this assessment and you can decide if Tableland Tutoring is right for your child. Phone to make an appointment and bring recent report cards with you.

Please feel free to contact me if you have any queries.
