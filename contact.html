Robyn Walmsley

"Teacher, Principal, Guidance Officer and Learning Support Teacher for over 20 years"

My name is Robyn Walmsley and I have been a Teacher, Principal, Guidance Officer and Learning Support Teacher for over 20 years.

I have a Masters Degree in Learning Support and I have a passion for education and expertise in identifying barriers to learning.

I retired in 2013 and started my own consultancy business, taking advantage of having more time on my hands to develop and refine a range of resources which I have been using successfully for many years.

My resources are very reasonably priced as I know education budgets are stretched and most teachers invest their own money to supplement their teaching. Many of my resources are free.

I believe in making learning fun and have many games which target specific skill deficits. I have also included screeners which help you identify entry-level skills and to track progress. My Literacy Intervention Handbook is designed to help struggling readers. All my resources are perfect for RTI (Response to Intervention) and they would very easily adapt to Home Schooling. Many of the maths resources are ACARA aligned for busy teachers.

The full range of my resources are available on Teachers Pay Teachers, available here. Some of my free resources can be found on the resources page.I am constantly reviewing my resources so follow me and get updates and freebies as they happen.
