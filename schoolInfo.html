School Referrals

"A new service for all schools on the Atherton Tablelands"

Your referral to me will generate a Student Learning Profile that will include practical classroom strategies your busy classroom teachers can use immediately to provide differentiation. I can meet with support staff and provide teacher aide training if required.

Please feel free to contact me if you have any queries.

  • School Fees 2014 -
    $770
  • Meeting and interview with parents/carers
  • Interview with child’s teacher and collection of school academic data
  • Interview with school’s Guidance Officer
  • Assessments as required, but could include:
    • YORK Assessment of Reading
    • Diagnostic Maths Tasks
    • WIAT-II (Abbreviated – Australian)
    • NNAT assessment of cognitive problem solving ability
    • Dyslexia Screening Test
  • A detailed report with:
    • Data collection and analysis
    • Recommendations
